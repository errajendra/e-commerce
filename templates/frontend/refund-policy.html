Refund Policy - {% site_title %}

Effective Date: 20 Dec 2024

At {% site_title %}, we strive to ensure the satisfaction of our students. If, for any reason, you are not entirely satisfied with your course enrollment, we’re here to help.

1. Course Enrollment Cancellation Policy

You have 7 days to cancel your course enrollment from the date you enrolled. To be eligible for a refund, you must not have accessed any course materials or completed any course assignments.

2. Refunds

Once we receive your cancellation request, we will notify you that we have received your request. We will immediately notify you on the status of your refund after verifying that you have not accessed any course materials.

If your refund is approved, we will initiate a refund to your original payment method within 3 working day's. You will receive the credit within a certain amount of days, depending on your card issuer's policies.

3. Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven’t received a refund yet, first check your bank account again. Then contact your credit card company, as it may take some time before your refund is officially posted.

Next, contact your bank. There is often some processing time before a refund is posted. If you’ve done all of this and you still have not received your refund, please contact us at {% support_mail %}.

4. Exceptions

Some courses are non-refundable, including but not limited to:

  • Courses that have already been accessed or completed
  • Courses with certificates or other digital credentials
  • Special promotion or discounted courses
